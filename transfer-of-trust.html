03
Fear Killer
🔑 Most Important Asset
What Changes. What Doesn't.
Jordan + Taylor · Conversational · 2–3 min
▼
The most strategically important piece in this entire campaign. The #1 student fear is: "My coaching experience is going to change for the worse." This video kills that fear directly and specifically. Jordan and Taeler are talking to each other — not performing for camera — which makes every claim land with more weight.
Format
Two-shot conversational · Natural back-and-forth · No teleprompter
Length
2–3 min — paced like a real conversation
Tone
Direct, specific, trust-building — each claim gets a reason
Push Date
May 6–7 · Pin on profile
Structure — Address Each Fear Directly
What doesn't change: The coaching methodology, the IFCA curriculum, the coaching team, the community, the results standard
What gets better: Taeler's operational focus, new resources FCA brings, fresh energy and systems
Jordan explicitly endorses Taeler's coaching philosophy — their alignment on client results
Taeler commits to specific things: maintaining the curriculum integrity, keeping the team, investing in product improvement
Close: "If you chose IFCA because of what we stand for — that's exactly what Taeler is here to protect and grow"

Student FAQ · Send May 17 (Closes TOT Campaign)
Your Questions About the Transition — Answered
FROM: Jordan + Taeler · TO: All Active Students
Proactive FAQ that addresses the questions students have been asking (or thinking). Pull real questions from community comments, replies, and DMs. Authenticity of the questions makes the answers land harder.
"Will my success coach change?" — answer specifically, with names if possible
"Will the curriculum change?" — Taeler answers this one personally
"What happens to my contract / payment plan?" — specific and reassuring
"Is Jordan still involved in any capacity?" — honest answer, no vagueness
"Can I still reach Jordan?" — set expectations clearly, warmly
"What's the biggest change I'll actually notice?" — tell them something real, not just reassurances
