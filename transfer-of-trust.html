IFCA · May 2026 · Announcement

After 11 Years,
We're Handing
The Keys Over.

Her name is Taeler De Haes. Here's everything you need to know — who she is, why we chose her, and what it means for you.

📹 Embed Announcement Video Here
Jordan + Erin + Taeler · Columbus Shoot · 3–4 min

Your Questions. Answered.

Everything we anticipated you'd want to know — answered directly.
Will my success coach change? ▼
No. Your current success coach is staying. The entire IFCA coaching team remains in place through and beyond the transition. Kevin Hamidi continues as Head Success Coach. Nothing in your day-to-day coaching experience changes.
What happens to my contract and payment plan? ▼
Every contract and payment plan is fully honored. This is a legal condition of the acquisition — your enrollment terms are protected exactly as written. No changes to your billing schedule, amounts, or any enrollment terms. If you want your specific contract confirmed in writing, reply to the student email and our team will send it.
Is Jordan still involved? ▼
Yes — through a 90-day transition period running through approximately Q3 2026. Jordan and Erin are actively involved during this window: calls, content, community. After Q3, Taeler leads fully. Jordan will be available on the June 11 webinar for live Q&A.
Will the curriculum change? ▼
The core IFCA Accelerator curriculum is not changing. What's being added over time: Taeler's FCA systems integrated to strengthen what's already there. The system you enrolled in is the system you're completing. We add — we don't subtract.
Who is Taeler De Haes and why should I trust her? ▼
Taeler is the founder of Fitness Coach Academy (FCA). She started with $825 in her bank account and generated $250,000 in her first year as an online coach — using the same systems IFCA teaches. She's not a business buyer. She's a coach who built exactly what you're building. Jordan and Erin spent two years vetting candidates. She was the right one.
What's the biggest change I'll actually notice? ▼
New energy and new faces at the top. Taeler is a hands-on leader — she'll be in the community, on calls, and accessible. FCA students are joining the IFCA ecosystem, which expands the peer community around you. The day-to-day experience gets fuller — not thinner.
Why did Jordan and Erin decide to sell? ▼
Not burnout. Inflection point. Growing IFCA to where it deserves to go requires someone whose only full focus is this company. Taeler is that person. This was a deliberate, multi-year process of vetting and relationship-building — not a reactive decision. The full story is in the announcement podcast. Listen to it.
Is this going to become a bigger, more corporate company? ▼
No. Taeler is a solo founder who built FCA the same way Jordan and Erin built IFCA — close to clients, close to community. She's not PE-backed. Not building a brand roll-up. The vision is a tighter, more focused company with better infrastructure. The core values — Relentless Growth, Extreme Ownership, Authentic Connection — those don't change. That's the standard Taeler is operating from.

FCA × IFCA · A New Era · May 2026

I Just Acquired
The #1 Fitness
Coaching Platform.

Taeler De Haes — founder of FCA — has acquired Impact Fitness Coaching Academy. Here's what happened, why, and what it means for you.

📹 Embed Announcement Video Here
Taeler · Columbus Shoot · 3–4 min

What FCA Students Are Asking.

Your most common questions — answered directly by Taeler.
Is FCA still going to exist? ▼
The FCA community and culture you know is being carried forward — but FCA as a standalone offer will be retired over the coming months. You'll transition into the IFCA ecosystem, which is a significant upgrade in resources, curriculum, and coaching support. Think of it as a graduation, not a discontinuation.
What happens to my current FCA contract? ▼
Every FCA contract is fully honored. Your terms, your payment schedule, your enrollment — all protected as-is. I personally oversaw this condition as part of the acquisition. You're not being renegotiated or pushed to upgrade until you're ready.
Will I still have access to Taeler? ▼
Yes — and more so. I'm leading IFCA, which means I'm more present, not less. I'll be in the community, on calls, and accessible in ways that a solo founder splitting time across two businesses simply can't be. This acquisition is what allows me to show up fully for you.
What is IFCA and why does it matter to me? ▼
IFCA — Impact Fitness Coaching Academy — has been running 11 years and has trained 4,000+ health, fitness, and nutrition coaches. 590+ quit their jobs. 650+ hit $10K/month. 170+ hit $40K/month. It's the most systemized fitness coaching education platform in the industry. I didn't buy it to change it — I bought it to lead it and bring it to the FCA community.
What's different about IFCA compared to FCA? ▼
Scale and infrastructure. IFCA has been running 11 years with a full coaching team, curriculum built on 4,000+ client data points, and a back-end path that takes coaches from their first client to 7-figure businesses. FCA's culture and relationship-first approach meets IFCA's systemization. The combination is what I designed this for.
Why did Taeler acquire IFCA now? ▼
Because the timing was right and the fit was undeniable. I've studied IFCA's systems for years. When Jordan and Erin approached me, I recognized what they'd built — and I knew that merging FCA's community with IFCA's infrastructure was a category-defining move. This wasn't opportunistic. It was the right next step for every FCA student's future.
What's next — what should I do right now? ▼
Watch the announcement video above. Listen to the story podcast — it's the full, unscripted version with all three of us. And mark your calendar for June 11 at 7pm ET: I'm hosting a live webinar where Jordan and Erin will be on for Q&A. Bring every question you have. That's where we go deep on what this means for you specifically.
